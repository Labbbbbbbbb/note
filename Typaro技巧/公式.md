> ### 行省略号`\cdots`，列省略号`\vdots`，斜向省略号（左上至右下）`\ddots`

```latex
$$
 \left\{
 \begin{matrix}
 1      & 2        & \cdots & 5        \\
 6      & 7        & \cdots & 10       \\
 \vdots & \vdots   & \ddots & \vdots   \\
 \alpha & \alpha+1 & \cdots & \alpha+4 
 \end{matrix}
 \right\}
$$
```
